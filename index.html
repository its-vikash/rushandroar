The International Automotive Culture Festival (IACF) proudly presents the Rush & Roar Show, an exhilarating platform for automotive enthusiasts, industry leaders, and brands to come together and celebrate the passion for cars, bikes, and innovations.

This isn’t just another auto show - It’s a comprehensive experience, featuring high- octane competitions, exclusive launches, creative showcases, and dynamic cultural attractions. IACF Rush & Roar Show unites the automotive community, providing a platform for enthusiasts, collectors, and businesses to connect, engage, and grow.

Why Attend?

Unique Selling Points

Direct Engagement with Collectors

  • For the first time, brands can interact directly with collectors and high-spending automotive enthusiasts.
  • This is a unique opportunity to engage with individuals eager to invest in premium vehicles and products.

A Dynamic Audience

  • The event draws a diverse crowd that includes car enthusiasts, industry leaders, potential investors, and influencers, making it a fertile ground for networking and collaboration.

A One-Stop Solution for Brands

  • IACF provides a comprehensive platform for brands, vendors, and service providers to showcase their offerings, ensuring maximum visibility and engagement.

Unmatched Excitement and Entertainment

  • From adrenaline-pumping speed shows to high-energy performances and interactive exhibits, IACF guarantees an unforgettable experience that keeps attendees engaged.

Massive Media Exposure

  • Designed to generate billions of views, the event offers unparalleled promotional opportunities, attracting top-tier media coverage and amplifying your brand’s reach.
